Synthesys... The Leading Voiceover and Video Production Platform

Synthesys platform was developed by season voice and video actors together with linguistic and AI professionals who recognized the need for an on-demand TTS and TTV production platform. Their goal was simple: create a solution that offers:

  • One stop shopping for both text-to-speech and text-to-video capabilities
  • All the tools required, no third party or additional post-production software required
  • Cloud access: no programs to install or update
  • Best-in-class assets with an extensive library of avatars and voices

  • Continuous AI improvements; keeps getting better and better
