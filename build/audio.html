Synthesys is the next generation text-to-speech realistic technology that generates a life-like human voice reading your text. Easy-to-use, Synthesys offers a wide range of male and female voices, tones, languages, and reading speeds, allowing you to produce enthusiast radio commercials, friendly greetings, documentary-style messages, tutorials, podcasts . . . anywhere you need text to voiceovers for your digital content.
Text-to-Voiceover in 1,2,3
1
Choose Your Voice
Select the ambassador you like best from among our extensive library of professional voices. You’ll choose gender, accent, style, and tone to represent you and your brand.
2
Enter Your Text
Either paste or type your text into the ai voice generator interface. Choose your reading speed and pause length between sentences. Now’s the time to make any last-minute edits to make sure your message is on-brand.
3
Click «Create»
Within seconds your new voice will render and ready for review. Listen to it carefully, and imagine you’re hearing this message as customer. Chances are, it’s spot on, but if not, make your adjustments and re-render your text to voiceover.
